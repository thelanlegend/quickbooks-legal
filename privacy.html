Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the QuickBooks MCP Integration ("Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize the Service, we access the following types of QuickBooks data:

  • Company information
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Invoice and billing data
  • Account and transaction information
  • Employee records (if authorized)

3. How We Use Information

The Service accesses your QuickBooks data solely to:

  • Display and manage accounting information through the MCP interface
  • Create, update, and manage invoices, customers, vendors, and other records
  • Facilitate authorized business operations

4. Data Storage and Security

The Service operates as a local integration tool. Your QuickBooks data is:

  • Accessed directly from QuickBooks Online via secure API connections
  • Not stored permanently by the Service
  • Protected by OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your QuickBooks data to third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Intuit/QuickBooks (as required for API functionality)
  • Authorized users within your organization

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Service's access to your QuickBooks account at any time
  • Request information about what data has been accessed
  • Disconnect the integration through QuickBooks Online settings

7. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are stored locally for the duration of your authorization. When you revoke access, all stored credentials are invalidated.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the system administrator.
